Ramapo C&T Club

The Computer and Technology Club is an organization founded to provide intellectual stimulation and serve as an information network of individuals interested in the broad spectrum of technology, including computers, information systems, networks, security, and software development. We invite speakers from faculty and industry to provide perspectives on the versatility of Computer Science and Information Systems/Technology.
